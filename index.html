Blackjack Rules

Objective: Beat the dealer by having a hand value closest to 21 without going over.


Card Values

  • Number cards: Face value.
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points each.
  • Ace (A): 1 or 11 points, whichever benefits your hand the most.

Gameplay

  • The dealer must stand on 17 or higher and hit on 16 or lower.
  • You may choose to "Hit" to draw an additional card or "Stand" to keep your current hand.
  • If your hand exceeds 21, you bust and lose the round.

Good luck and have fun!
